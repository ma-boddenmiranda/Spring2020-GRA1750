About

Located in the South of Manhattan (SoHo), we bring our authentic and high quality
pizza to all the pie lovers in New York. Prince Street Pizza has been around since
2012 and provides the classic taste to what a New York style pizza represents,
while maintaining the use of the freshest ingredients. Our mouth watering menu is positive
to make you want to join the Prince Street family forever.
