About

Boy meets girl. Boy falls in love. Girl doesn’t. This post modern love story is never what we expect it to be — it’s thorny yet
exhilarating, funny and sad, a twisted journey of highs and lows that doesn’t quite go where we think it will. When Tom, a
hapless greeting card copywriter and hopeless romantic, is blindsided after his girlfriend Summer dumps him, he shifts back
and forth through various periods of their 500 days “together” to try to figure out where things went wrong.
His reflections ultimately lead him to finally rediscover his true passions in life.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, and Drama
